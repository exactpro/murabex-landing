ABOUT MURABEX

Built for Islamic Finance

Murabex is an enterprise-grade platform purpose-built for Sharia-compliant Murabaha transactions. It connects banks, liquidity providers, asset issuers and customers in a unified ecosystem.

The architecture combines a high-performance Rust-based quote engine with a reactive Java backend and a modern trading UI — delivering institutional-grade speed, compliance and usability out of the box.

Murabex is built by Exactpro Systems, a company with deep expertise in independent AI-driven financial technology testing and platform engineering.

Agreement Lifecycle

Liquidity Provider Scoring

Distributed Ledger & Settlement
